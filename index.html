Guide to Production Success

Course Overview

Whether you’re an early career professional looking to get into the production industry or an established veteran looking to make a career change, this course has something to offer everyone.  You’ll learn the the right type of people to surround yourself with and the proper mindset to adopt at the outset; the importance of embracing change and the need to evaluate your current skill set; understanding your potential employer and ways to switch career paths; what happens when you get stuck in a job; the essential qualities for success and much more!  Joe goes through each lesson carefully step by step, and talks to you like you’re hanging out backstage at a gig without the traditional classroom formality – Just get ready to make some lists!  “Creativity takes courage,” as Joe likes to say, so there’s no time to waste. 

About the Guide

Joe Scacciaferro

Joe Scacciaferro's career is a testament to his exceptional blend of music, technology, and multimedia expertise. Starting as a musician in the vibrant club scenes of New York City and New Jersey, Joe later ventured into video production after producing a song performed by Ray Charles in a music video dedicated to his daughter with Down Syndrome. He founded Ferro Productions, excelling in documentaries, live broadcasts, and music scoring. His work thus far has earned him five Emmys and nine nominations and two Super Bowl rings.
